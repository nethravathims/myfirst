

Welcom to myfirst cooking recipe: Pasta


Ingredients: 
Pasta Penne
olive oil 
garlic
seasoning 
salt
pepper

Preparing Method:
-Boil the pasta for 12 to 15 minutes.
-Add 2 sponns of oil in a pan, garlic and fry untill golden brown.
-Add optional veggeis if you need (tomatoes, paprika etc.)
-Transfer boiled pasta to the pan and add the seasoning, salt, pepper and some chilly flakes.
-leave for few min closing the lid and your simple veg pasta is ready to sevre!!
